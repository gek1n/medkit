2. Data we process

Data you enter yourself (stored locally on your device)

  • Profile: name, date of birth (optional), family member profiles;
  • Health data (special category data): medication names, dosages, schedules, allergies, chronic conditions, vaccinations, surgeries/hospitalizations, lab results, doctor appointments, wellbeing check-ins, photos and documents (prescriptions, discharge summaries, certificates).

Technical data (may pass through our server temporarily)

  • Random technical identifiers for your device and sync channel;
  • Device push token (Firebase Cloud Messaging);
  • Technical request metadata (timestamp, payload size) — used for rate limiting.

We do not collect usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or data for profiling.

4. Core principle: a blind server

No accounts — nothing to leak

MedKit has no sign-up, no email/password, no social login, and no central user database. This is a deliberate architectural choice: if there are no accounts, there's no risk of a user-database breach.

Where the app does need a server (syncing between your own devices, voice commands), the server only ever sees encrypted data and technical identifiers — the decryption key exists only on your devices. The server never sees, in readable form, your name, medication names, dosages, schedules, photos, or health status.

📱

5. Data stored only on your device

Everything you enter in MedKit is stored locally only, in an encrypted database on your device. Photos and documents are encrypted separately. Encryption keys are stored in your operating system's secure hardware storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android) and never leave the device in plaintext.

App access can optionally be further protected by biometrics (Face ID / fingerprint) or your device passcode.

Consequence: if you uninstall the app without first creating a backup, your data is lost permanently. We keep no server-side copy we could restore on your behalf.

7. AI features and third-party providers

Voice commands and prescription scanning — Anthropic (Claude)

If you use voice input or prescription/package scanning, the relevant text or photo is sent to our server, which forwards it to Anthropic, PBC's (USA) Claude model for recognition. Before first use of each of these features, the app explicitly asks for separate consent and names Anthropic as the provider. The photo is used only for recognition and is not stored anywhere afterward. Free-text descriptions of wellbeing or symptoms are never sent to the cloud.

Anthropic acts as our data processor and handles these requests under its own privacy policy and a data processing agreement with us.

No automated decisions without your involvement

AI recognition results (medication name, dosage, reference information) are only proposed to you for review — the app never saves or applies them automatically without you reviewing and confirming the data yourself. This does not constitute automated decision-making with legal or similarly significant effects within the meaning of Art. 22 GDPR.

Push notifications — Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google)

To deliver reminders, the app uses Firebase Cloud Messaging. Google receives only your device's push token and an encrypted payload it cannot read.

International data transfers. Anthropic and Google process data on servers outside Ukraine/the EEA (including in the United States). Such transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards — Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission, or an equivalent mechanism provided for in our agreement with the provider.

8. Backups (Google Drive / iCloud)

You may optionally create a backup in your own Google Drive (Android) or iCloud (iOS), written to a hidden, app-private storage area.

Data in the backup is already encrypted the same way it is on your device, and additionally protected by a password you choose yourself. Neither MedKit, Google, nor Apple can read the backup's contents without that password — and we never store it anywhere. If you forget it, the backup cannot be recovered. You manage your backups yourself — storage and deletion happen in your own cloud storage, outside our control.

9. Family sharing and dependent profiles

Sync between family devices

To share data between your own devices or a family member's device, the devices exchange a one-time code. Data is encrypted with a key derived from that code and decrypted only on the device that entered the same code. The server only temporarily holds the encrypted blob — it cannot be read without the code.

The "hide my medical card from this family member" setting hides the data in that member's interface. Please note: if your device and that member's device are already synced as a family, the data is physically transferred and stored (in encrypted form) on both devices — hiding it in the interface is not a device-level access barrier.

Children's and other dependents' profiles

MedKit lets you add profiles for family members (e.g. children or elderly parents) managed by the device owner. These are not separate accounts — dependent profiles have no login of their own and no direct access to the app unless the profile owner sets that up themselves.

Data in a child's profile is entered and controlled by the parent/guardian exercising parental responsibility, who is responsible for its accuracy and appropriateness. We do not address children directly and do not offer the app as a service directed at children.

11. Your rights

Since the bulk of your data lives only on your device, most rights are exercised directly in the app. Under GDPR and the Ukrainian Law "On Personal Data Protection", you have the right to:

  • Access your data — view it directly in the app, or use "Data export" in your profile for a readable copy;
  • Rectification of inaccurate data — edit records directly;
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") — delete individual records, family member profiles, or the whole app;
  • Restriction of, and objection to, processing — disable the relevant feature (e.g. AI recognition) in settings;
  • Data portability — export your data in a structured format via "Data export";
  • Withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing;
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority — in Ukraine, the Ukrainian Parliament Commissioner for Human Rights; for EU residents, the supervisory authority of your country of residence.

For transient server-side technical data (pairing blobs, push tokens), email us at privacy@elly-medkit.com — most of it expires automatically within minutes or hours regardless.

12. Security and breach notification

We apply technical and organizational measures appropriate to the sensitivity of health data: encryption of data at rest and in transit, encryption keys stored in your device's secure hardware storage, optional biometric protection for app access, encrypted server communication (HTTPS/TLS), and rate limiting against abuse.

In the event of a personal data breach that creates a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority and, where required, you personally, without undue delay, as required by applicable law.
